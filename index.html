FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

We strongly recommend booking in advance, especially for bridal services, makeup, and spa treatments. Walk-ins are welcome for quick services like threading, nail colors, and simple haircuts based on availability. You can book via our website form, WhatsApp, or phone call.

What products do you use for hair coloring?

We use only premium international brands including L'Oréal Professionnel, Schwarzkopf, and Wella Professionals. All our color products are carefully selected for safety, vibrancy, and longevity — giving you the best results while maintaining hair health.

How early should I book for bridal services?

For bridal packages, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October–February). This allows time for a trial session, consultations, and pre-bridal treatments. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your complete bridal package.

Is a patch test required before coloring?

Yes, for first-time clients or those with sensitive skin/scalp, a patch test is recommended 48 hours before any coloring service. Our team will guide you through this during your consultation. Your safety and comfort are our highest priority.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, Bank Transfers (Jazzcash, Easypaisa, Bank Transfer), and Credit/Debit Cards. Please note: 3% sales tax applies on cash & bank transfers, and 10% on credit/debit card payments as per government regulations.

Do you offer home service or on-location makeup?

Yes! We offer on-location bridal and event makeup services in Karachi. Additional charges apply for travel and setup. Contact us on WhatsApp with your event date, location, and requirements for a custom quote. Advance booking required.

How long does a Hydra Facial take?

A standard Hydra Facial session takes approximately 60–75 minutes. We recommend arriving 10 minutes early to complete a brief skin consultation. For enhanced protocols with additional boosters or mask treatments, sessions may take up to 90 minutes.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. For bridal packages and special event bookings, 48-72 hours notice is required. Last-minute cancellations for premium services may incur a small booking fee. We appreciate your understanding.
